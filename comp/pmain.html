Geotechnics is a group of engineering consultants specialized in combined Geotechnical, Structural and Marine Projects.

We bring innovative solutions to our clients through the wide experience of our consultants in all disciplines supported by strong research background and constant ties to engineering Universities. Most of our staff have advanced degrees in Engineering and a number of them have professor chairs and members of engineering code comities.

Our design philosophy stand firm on value engineering. Goal of value engineering is to maximize the value of the design through a systematic and rigorous process. Design alternatives are reached through accurate field work, sufficient data collection and employing the most recent and advanced analysis methods. Afterwards, design alternatives are evaluated for cost and life expectancy. Through value engineering the cost components of the design is analyzed and optimized to provide the required lifetime of the design in the best economic fashion.
